Chicken Fried Rice

Introduction

Chicken fried rice is a popular dish in Chinese cuisine. It is made from steamed rice stir-fried in a wok, often with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, and meat. It is sometimes served as the penultimate dish in Chinese banquets, just before dessert. As a homemade dish, chicken fried rice is typically made with leftover ingredients from other dishes, leading to countless variations.

Ingredients

1. 2 cups of rice
2. 1/2 cup of chicken
3. 1/2 cup of eggs
4. 1/2 cup of vegetables
5. 1/2 cup of soy sauce
6. 1/2 cup of salt
7. 1/2 cup of black pepper
8. 1/2 cup of green onions
9. 1/2 cup of garlic
10. 1/2 cup of ginger

Following are the steps to make chicken fried rice

1. In a pan, add rice, water, and cook until done.
2. Now, in a pan, add chicken, eggs, vegetables, soy sauce, salt, black pepper, green onions, garlic, ginger, and mix it well.
3. Now, add cooked rice and mix it well.
4. Your Chicken Fried Rice is ready to serve.
