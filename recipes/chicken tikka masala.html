Chicken Tikka Masala

Introduction

Chicken tikka masala is a dish of chunks of roasted marinated chicken in a spiced curry sauce. The sauce is usually creamy and orange-coloured. There are multiple claims to its place of origin, including the Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent or Glasgow in Scotland.

Ingredients

1. 1/2 cup of yogurt
2. 1/4 cup of vinegar
3. 1/4 cup of olive oil
4. 1 kg of chicken
5. 1/2 cup of salt
6. 1/2 cup of black pepper
7. 1/2 cup of red chili powder
8. 1/2 cup of garam masala
9. 1/2 cup of ginger
10. 1/2 cup of garlic

Following are the steps to make chicken tikka masala

1. In a bowl, add yogurt, vinegar, olive oil, chicken, salt, black pepper, red chili powder, garam masala, ginger, garlic, and mix it well.
2. Now, in a pan, add marinated chicken and cook until done.
3. Now, your Chicken Tikka Masala is ready to serve.
