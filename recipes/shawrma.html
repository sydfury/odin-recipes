Shawrma

]

Introduction

Shawrma is a Middle Eastern dish of garlicky meat or poultry served on pitas. It is a popular street food in the Middle East and around the world

Ingredients

1. 1/2 cup plain yogurt
2. 1/4 cup vinegar
3. 1/4 cup olive oil
4. 1 kg chicken salt
5. 1 roti
6. 1/2 cup mayonnaise
7. 1/2 cup ketchup
8. 1/2 cup chili garlic sauce
9. 1/2 cup cabbage
10. 1/2 cucumber

Following are the steps to make shawrma

1. In a bowl, add plain yogurt, vinegar, olive oil, chicken, salt, and mix it well.
2. Now, in a pan, add marinated chicken and cook until done.
3. Now, in a roti, add mayonnaise, ketchup, chili garlic sauce, cabbage, cucumber, cooked chicken, and wrap it.
4. Your Shawrma is ready to serve.
